NOTES FOR CARD CREATION:
===========================================

STAT EXPLANATION:
- Base Stats are the primary combat stats used in battles
- Detailed Abilities are the core character attributes that define their nature
- All stats range from roughly 30-200 depending on the character's focus
- Higher rarity characters generally have higher total stat values

ARCHETYPE MEANINGS:
- Warrior: Melee combat specialists
- Leader: Command and inspire others  
- Mystic: Magic and supernatural powers
- Trickster: Agile and unpredictable
- Beast: Raw physical power and instinct
- Plus various specialized archetypes for unique characters

RARITY LEVELS (in order):
1. Common (basic characters)
2. Uncommon (Billy the Kid, Sam Spade)
3. Rare (Agent X, Frankenstein, Robin Hood, Holmes, Zeta)
4. Epic (Cleopatra, Joan of Arc, Space Cyborg, Tesla)
5. Legendary (Achilles, Dracula, Fenrir, Genghis Khan)
6. Mythic (Merlin, Sun Wukong) - highest tier
