# Power Level Categorization
## Manual Review of All 240 Signature Powers + 314 Spells

**Cost Formula:**
- Base Unlock Cost: `Tier×2 + PowerLevel - 1`
- Rank Up Costs: `UnlockCost + (Rank×Tier)`

### Tier 4 (Signature) Cost Structure:
- Level 1: Unlock=8, Rank2=12, Rank3=16
- Level 2: Unlock=9, Rank2=13, Rank3=17
- Level 3: Unlock=10, Rank2=14, Rank3=18

## SIGNATURE POWERS (Tier 4) - 240 Total

### Achilles (7 powers)

**Level 1 (Weak - Basic utility/modest buffs):**
- `achilles_legendary_warrior` - Passive physical attack buff (10/22/40%) + lifesteal at R3
- `achilles_divine_heritage` - Heal + defense buff (moderate)

**Level 2 (Medium - Strong utility/buffs):**
- `achilles_invulnerability` - Damage reduction (8/18/30%) + crit immunity at R3
- `achilles_swift_footed` - Speed buff (12/25/45%) + double attack at R3
- `achilles_spear_of_peleus` - Direct damage (30/70/130) + unblockable + pierce at R3

**Level 3 (Strong - Game-changing abilities):**
- `achilles_wrath` - Massive damage (25/55/100%) + speed + cannot miss at R3
- `achilles_heros_stand` - Conditional all-stat buff (40/70/120%) + cannot die at R3

### Agent X (8 powers)

**Level 1 (Weak):**
- `agent_x_shadow_cloak` - Untargetable (1-2 turns) + evasion buff
- `agent_x_disguise` - Redirect attacks / become untargetable

**Level 2 (Medium):**
- `agent_x_assassination_training` - Conditional damage buff (15/35/60%) vs healthy targets
- `agent_x_precision_strike` - Damage (35/80/150) + cannot miss + defense reduction
- `agent_x_bleed_out` - Damage (40/90/160) + grievous wound + bleed + max HP reduction at R3

**Level 3 (Strong):**
- `agent_x_execute` - Execute below HP threshold (20/30/40%) + high damage
- `agent_x_deep_cover` - Undetectable + massive next attack bonus (150/230/340%)
- `agent_x_ghost_protocol` - Untargetable + massive damage buff (30/65/120%) + ignore armor + cannot miss at R3

### Aleister Crowley (7 powers)

**Level 1 (Weak):**
- `crowley_mountain_climber` - Stamina + exhaustion resist + HP regen
- `crowley_scandalous_reputation` - Crit resist + enemy accuracy debuff

**Level 2 (Medium):**
- `crowley_psychological_warfare` - Fear + confusion debuff (single target → AoE at R3)
- `crowley_opium_trance` - Evasion (40/65/95%) + see enemy moves + counter chance
- `crowley_worldly_knowledge` - Weakness exploit + bonus vs all types

**Level 3 (Strong):**
- `crowley_unbreakable_will` - Control immunity (60/85/100%) + reflect mental attacks at R3
- `crowley_cult_leader` - Charm/convert enemies (1-2 targets) with loyalty boost
