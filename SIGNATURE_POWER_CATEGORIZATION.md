# Signature Power Level Categorization
## 231 Powers Across 33 Characters (7 powers each)

**Pyramid Structure per Character:**
- **Level 1 (Common):** 4 powers - basic/utility abilities
- **Level 2 (Uncommon):** 2 powers - solid combat abilities
- **Level 3 (Rare):** 1 power - ultimate/game-changing ability

**Cost Formula (Tier 4 Signature):**
- Level 1: Unlock=8, Rank2=12, Rank3=16
- Level 2: Unlock=9, Rank2=13, Rank3=17
- Level 3: Unlock=10, Rank2=14, Rank3=18
